Frequently Asked Questions

What families ask first.

Is this a vacation or a bootcamp?

Both. Students attend structured instruction during the mornings. The remainder of each day is yours to enjoy together.

Do parents attend classes?

No. Parent workshops are optional and designed specifically for families navigating college admissions.

How many students are accepted?

Enrollment is intentionally limited to maintain a low student-to-instructor ratio.

Is the cruise included?

Families reserve cruise accommodations through our group booking. Educational tuition is purchased separately.
