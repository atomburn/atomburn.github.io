Norwegian Bliss from Long Beach

Embarking and disembarking in Long Beach on Norwegian Cruise Line, this first sailing gives Southern California families a full week of learning, port time, and vacation without airport logistics.

  • Freestyle dining, casual meals, and specialty restaurants including Cagney's Steakhouse and La Cucina.
  • The Waterfront oceanfront promenade for family walks, sunset views, and outdoor dining.
  • Pool deck, mini golf, Ocean Loops, Bliss Speedway, and ship activities between class blocks.
  • Entertainment including The Choir of Man, lounges, shows, and evening family time.
  • Port days in Cabo San Lucas, Mazatlan, and Puerto Vallarta with optional excursions and local exploring.
  • Two at-sea days that protect the SAT bootcamp rhythm without sacrificing vacation time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What families ask first.

Is this a vacation or a bootcamp?

Both. Students attend structured instruction during the mornings. The remainder of each day is yours to enjoy together.

Do parents attend classes?

No. Parent workshops are optional and designed specifically for families navigating college admissions.

How many students are accepted?

Enrollment is intentionally limited to maintain a low student-to-instructor ratio.

Is the cruise included?

Families reserve cruise accommodations through our group booking. Educational tuition is purchased separately.
